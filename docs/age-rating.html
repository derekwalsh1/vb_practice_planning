Content Rating

Ace Your Plans has been designed as a productivity tool for volleyball coaches and has received a 4+ age rating from Apple, indicating it's suitable for all ages.

Why 4+?

The app contains no objectionable content and is designed purely for sports practice planning. It features:

  • No violence or aggressive content
  • No mature themes or suggestive content
  • No profanity or crude humor
  • No realistic violence or horror themes
  • No alcohol, tobacco, or drug references
  • No gambling or contests
  • No user-generated content or social features
  • No advertisements
  • No in-app purchases

Privacy & Safety

Child Safety

While the app is suitable for all ages, it's primarily designed for adult coaches. The app does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Require account creation or login
  • Connect to social media
  • Allow communication with other users
  • Display advertisements
  • Include external links (except in settings/help)

Parental Guidance

Although the app has a 4+ rating, it is designed as a professional tool for volleyball coaches. Parents and guardians should supervise young children if they use the app.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. All information is stored locally on the device. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
