Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add photos to my drills?

While creating or editing an activity, tap the "Image" tab. You can either take a photo with your camera or choose an existing photo from your library. The app will automatically save and compress the image for optimal performance.

Q: Can I share my practice plans with other coaches?

Yes! Go to Settings and tap "Export Plans" or "Export Activities". This creates a file that includes all your plans and embedded images. Share this file with other coaches, and they can import it into their app.

Q: Where is my data stored?

All your practice plans, activities, and images are stored locally on your device. Your data is never uploaded to any servers, ensuring complete privacy and offline access.

Q: What happens if I delete the app?

If you delete the app, all your data will be removed from your device. We recommend regularly exporting your plans as backups. You can then import them back if you reinstall the app.

Q: Can I use the app offline?

Absolutely! Ace Your Plans works completely offline. Since all data is stored locally, you don't need an internet connection to create, edit, or run your practice plans.

Q: How do I reorder activities in a practice plan?

When creating or editing a practice plan, simply tap and hold on an activity, then drag it to the desired position in the list.

Q: Can I edit an activity that's already in a plan?

Yes! When you edit an activity from the Activities tab, the changes will automatically appear in all practice plans that use that activity.

Q: Why can't I access my camera?

Make sure you've granted camera permissions to the app. Go to iOS Settings → Ace Your Plans → and enable Camera and Photos access.

Q: Does the app work on iPad?

Yes! Ace Your Plans is designed to work on both iPhone and iPad, taking advantage of the larger screen for better planning and visualization.

Q: How do I delete a practice plan or activity?

Swipe left on any plan or activity in the list view, then tap the delete button. You can also tap "Edit" in the top right corner to select multiple items for deletion.
