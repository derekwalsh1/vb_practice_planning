Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

Introduction

Ace Your Plans ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our volleyball practice planning mobile application.

Information We Collect

Ace Your Plans is designed with privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device.

  • Practice Plans: Your created practice plans, drills, and activities are stored locally on your device.
  • Photos: Any photos you add to drills are stored in your device's local storage.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect any analytics or usage data.

How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • We do not have access to your practice plans or any data you create
  • We do not transmit your data to our servers
  • We do not share your information with third parties
  • We do not use your data for marketing or advertising purposes

Data Storage and Security

All your information is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage mechanisms. Your data is only accessible through the app on your device.

Photo Access

The app requests access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to:

  • Take photos of drill diagrams
  • Select existing photos from your photo library

Photos are only stored locally on your device and are not uploaded or shared anywhere.

Import/Export Feature

When you export practice plans:

  • Data is exported as a file you control
  • You choose where to save or share the file
  • Images are embedded in the export file using base64 encoding
  • We do not receive or store your exported data

Children's Privacy

Ace Your Plans does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is designed for coaches and does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Support Page.
